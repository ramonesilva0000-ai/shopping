    Help & Support

    How does delivery work?

    We deliver same-day in Eldoret, Nairobi, Kisumu, Nakuru and Mombasa for orders placed before 2pm. Boda riders for under 10kg, vans for big shops. Live tracking on every order.

    What is Pamoja Cart?

    A shared cart for households or chamas. Start one, share the link, everyone adds from their phone. We pool the order to unlock bulk discounts.

    What is Lipa Pole Pole?

    Pay-later for items above KSh 5,000. Split the cost into 3 monthly payments via M-Pesa. Zero interest if you pay on time.

    How does Tuma Nyumbani work?

    Choose products, then in checkout select "Send to someone else" — enter their address and phone. Optionally record a voice note that we deliver with the package.

    Can I order without a smartphone?

    Yes. Dial *483*KCM# on any phone, or WhatsApp 0700 547 244. Our team will help you place the order.

    Talk to a human

    Call 0700 KIPCHIMATT, 7am–9pm daily. Or pop into any branch.
