    Help & Support

    How does delivery work?

    We deliver same-day in Eldoret, Nairobi, Kisumu, Nakuru and Mombasa for orders placed before 2pm. Next-day for outlying towns. Delivery is free above KSh 3,000.

    How do I pay?

    M-Pesa Express (STK push) is fastest. We also accept Visa/Mastercard and Cash on Delivery up to KSh 10,000.

    Can I return an item?

    Yes. Fresh produce within 24h, packaged goods within 7 days, electronics within 14 days. Bring the item plus the receipt to any branch.

    What is KipChim Rewards?

    You earn 1 point for every KSh 100 spent. 100 points = KSh 50 off. Redeem at checkout.

    Talk to a human

    Call 0700 KIPCHIMATT or WhatsApp 0700 547 244, 7am–9pm daily.
